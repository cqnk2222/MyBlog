---
title: 'The Value of Structured Notes'
description: 'A short reflection on why I want my notes to behave more like a handbook than a feed.'
pubDate: '2026-03-27'
---

When I learn a technical topic, a chronological stream of posts is usually not enough. It is too easy
for useful ideas to disappear into timestamps.

What I want instead is a structure that lets me return to a topic weeks later and still understand how
the pieces fit together. Notes should not only record what I read. They should reduce the cost of
re-entry.

That is why this part of the site is organized by section and chapter. It is closer to a handbook than
to a diary.
